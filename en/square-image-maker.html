Why Use a Square Maker Without Cropping?

On social platforms like Instagram, non-square images can sometimes be cut off in the grid view. By using this tool, you can keep your original photo intact while adding stylish padding to make it a perfect square fit for any post.

Key Features

  • Blurred Background: Create a sense of depth without distracting from the main subject.
  • Brightness Control: Darken the background to make the subject pop or brighten it for a clean look.
  • Privacy Focused: Your images are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to a server.
  • Bulk Conversion: Process multiple photos at once with the same settings to save time.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop images into the area above or click "Select Images."
  2. Choose a "Background Mode" and adjust the blur or color to your liking.
  3. Click the "Create Square Images" button to generate the new files.
  4. Click "Download this image" to save your work.
