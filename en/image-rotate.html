What is the Image Rotation Tool?

The "Image Rotation Tool" is a free online utility that allows you to easily change the orientation of photos and illustrations directly in your browser. Whether you have a smartphone photo that accidentally ended up sideways or an upside-down image, you can fix it instantly. No installation is required, and it works on both PC and mobile devices.

Benefits of This Tool

  • One-Click Rotation: Rotate 90 degrees left/right or 180 degrees instantly.
  • High-Quality Save: Processing is done within the browser, and you can save the result in high-quality PNG format.
  • Privacy Focused: Images are never sent to a server. Processing is completed entirely within your terminal (browser) for maximum security.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop an image into the area above, or click the button to select a file.
  2. Use the "Right 90°", "Left 90°", or "180°" buttons to adjust the image orientation.
  3. Once the orientation is correct, click the "Save Rotated Image" button to download.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I use this on iPhone or Android?

A. Yes! Since it runs in the browser, you can select images from your mobile photo library to rotate and save them directly.

Q. Can I rotate multiple images at once?

A. Currently, the tool processes one image at a time. This ensures you can verify the orientation with high precision before saving.
