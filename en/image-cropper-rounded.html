What is the Circle & Rounded Corner Crop Tool?

This is a simple online tool to crop images into perfect circles or rounded rectangles directly in your browser. It's perfect for creating SNS profile pictures or assets for website design. Your images are processed locally and are never sent to a server, ensuring your privacy.

Benefits of this Tool

  • Intuitive Drag Control: Adjust the crop area directly using your mouse or finger.
  • Completely Free & No Registration: Use it immediately without any tedious sign-up process.
  • Transparent PNG Support: Saves the cropped area with a transparent background, making it easy to use on any design.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop an image into the area above or select a file using the button.
  2. Choose between "Circle" or "Rounded" and adjust the size with the slider.
  3. Drag the highlighted area in the preview to the desired position.
  4. Click "Save as Transparent PNG" to download your result.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Can I crop a perfect circle?

A. Yes. By selecting the "Circle" shape and adjusting the size, you can easily crop any square or rectangular image into a clean circle.

Q. What happens to the area outside the rounded corners?

A. The area outside the crop is treated as "transparent." This is why we recommend saving in PNG format, which preserves transparency information.

Q. Is it suitable for SNS profile icons?

A. Absolutely. It is optimized for creating profile icons for platforms like WhatsApp, X (Twitter), and Instagram.

Q. Does the image quality degrade?

A. We maintain the original resolution as much as possible. Using browser Canvas features, the tool renders high-quality output comparable to professional editing software.

Q. Are my images uploaded to a server?

A. No. All processing happens within your browser (locally). Even sensitive photos or personal information can be edited safely.
