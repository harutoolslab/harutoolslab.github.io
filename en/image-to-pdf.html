What is the Image to PDF Converter?

This tool allows you to convert images (JPG, PNG, WebP, etc.) taken with your smartphone or digital camera into a universally compatible PDF format. By selecting multiple images, they will be merged into a single PDF file in the order of your choice. No app installation is required; all processing happens directly in your browser.

Common Use Cases

  • Submitting Documents: Convert photos of IDs, insurance cards, or resumes into professional PDFs.
  • Reports & Assignments: Capture handwritten notes and compile them into a single PDF document.
  • Digital Record Keeping: Save and manage receipts or invoices as PDF files.

Features of This Tool

1. Privacy-First Design

Your images are never uploaded to a server. Since the PDF is generated within your device's browser, you can safely convert sensitive documents without worry.

2. Automatic A4 Fit

Even with images of varying sizes, choosing the "Auto-adjust to A4" setting will ensure they are laid out correctly for standard document printing.

3. Easy Page Order Verification

Uploaded images are shown in a preview grid, allowing you to confirm the sequence before conversion. The order is determined by the sequence in which you select the files.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Is there a fee to use this tool?

A. No, it is completely free to use. There are no limits on the number of pages or file size.

Q. Can I merge multiple images into one PDF?

A. Yes. If you upload multiple images, they will be combined into a single PDF file with one image per page.

Q. Does the image quality change?

A. We maintain the original resolution as much as possible. Since it uses the browser's Canvas functionality, images are embedded into the PDF in a high-quality state.

Q. Is my data secure?

A. Very secure. This tool operates "offline" within your browser. Image data is never sent to our servers, so your confidential documents remain private.

Q. Does it work on smartphones?

A. Yes, you can use it directly from the browser on iPhone, Android, or tablet devices without installing any apps.
