What is Pixel Art Maker?

This tool is an online editor specialized in creating "Pixel Art" by placing colors precisely one square at a time. With a fixed background and light gray grid lines, even beginners can easily start drawing without getting lost.

Key Features

  • Completely Free: Start drawing instantly in your browser with no registration required.
  • Grid Support: Dynamic grid lines move with the zoom level, so you always know your pixel position.
  • High-Quality Export: Save your work as a transparent PNG, enlarged to maintain crisp pixel quality for social media or icons.

How to Use

  1. Choose your canvas size (e.g., 16x16) to prepare the workspace.
  2. Use the Pen tool and Color Picker to draw your art.
  3. Select the "Move" tool to drag and scroll the canvas.
  4. Use "Save Color" to store your favorite colors in the palette.
  5. Select your export size and click "Save Image" to download.
