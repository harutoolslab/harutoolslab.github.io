What is the Text Cleaner & Remover?

This tool is a browser-based text utility that allows you to remove unwanted elements from your text with one click. It is especially useful for fixing broken line breaks when copying from PDFs or batch-deleting specific symbols from data lists.

Main Features

  • Batch Line Break Removal: Instantly convert multi-line text into a single continuous line.
  • Space Removal: Removes all types of spaces (including half-width and full-width) from your text.
  • Custom Character Removal: Define any string, such as commas, tabs, or specific symbols, to be removed entirely.
  • Privacy Focused: Your text is never sent to a server. All processing happens locally within your browser.

How to Use

  1. Paste your text into the textarea above.
  2. Click "Remove Line Breaks" or "Remove Spaces" depending on your needs.
  3. To remove specific characters (e.g., symbols like * or ,), enter them in the input field and click "Remove Specific Text".
  4. Click "Copy Result" to get your cleaned text.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Will spaces between words be preserved if I remove line breaks?

A. The "Remove Line Breaks" button only removes new line codes. If you want to remove spaces as well, please click the "Remove Spaces" button afterwards.

Q. Can I remove multiple different characters at once?

A. Currently, the tool removes the exact string entered in the input field as a single target.

Q. Can I use this on a smartphone?

A. Yes, the tool is designed to work seamlessly on mobile and tablet browsers.
