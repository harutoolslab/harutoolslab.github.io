Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Are there hidden fees for utilizing these tools?

A. No, all utilities listed across HaruTools Lab are entirely free. There are no registration forms, pro features, or sudden paywalls.

Q. Do I need to create an account or sign in?

A. Not at all. Simply visit the tool's page and upload your assets to start working instantly. We do not gather personal identifiers or email accounts.

Q. Where are my files saved when I upload them?

A. They aren't. Our web architecture handles file rendering via client-side JavaScript. This means elements remain purely within your device sandbox and disappear once the browser tab is closed.

Q. Can I use the outputs for commercial distribution?

A. Yes. Any images, formatting corrections, or file outputs produced here are completely yours to use across blogs, social media distributions, print layout items, and software deployments.

Q. Does this site support mobile platforms?

A. Yes, every component features a flexible responsive grid. You can process your photography or check values via mobile or tablet devices just as you would on a desktop setup.

Q. What should I do if a tool fails to render or behaves unexpectedly?

A. Please verify that your web browser is updated to its latest release. Content blockers or strict private sandbox configurations may occasionally interfere with client-side canvas APIs. If issues persist, try executing the tool inside an alternate web browser.
