Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Is there any cost to use this service?

A. No, all tools are completely free to use. There are no hidden fees or premium plans for specific features.

Q. Is registration or login required?

A. Not at all. Simply visit the site, choose your tool, and start working. We do not collect any personal information like email addresses.

Q. Where are my uploaded files saved?

A. They aren't saved anywhere. Our tools use JavaScript to process files within your browser, so files are never transferred to a server. Once processing is finished, the data exists only on your local device.

Q. Can I use the processed results for commercial purposes?

A. Yes. You are free to use them for business documents, blog content, social media posts, product packaging, and any other professional scenario.

Q. Does it work on smartphones (iPhone/Android)?

A. Yes, every tool is responsive and provides full functionality on mobile and tablet browsers just like the desktop version.

Q. What should I do if a tool doesn't display correctly?

A. Please ensure your browser is updated to the latest version. Sometimes "Private Mode" or browser extensions (like ad blockers) may interfere with the scripts. If the issue persists, please try a different browser.
