Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Is there any cost?

A. No, all tools on HaruTools Lab are completely free. We are supported by advertisements, so there are no hidden fees.

Q. Are my uploaded files saved on the server?

A. No. Our tools process everything on the "client-side" (your browser). Your data stays on your device, and privacy is fully protected.

Q. Can I use it on my smartphone?

A. Yes, all tools are mobile-responsive and work on iPhone, Android, and tablets without any installation.

Q. Can I process multiple images at once?

A. Most tools currently support processing one file at a time. We are working on updates to support batch processing in the future.

Q. Will the quality of the image decrease?

A. Our tools are optimized to maintain the highest possible quality. While some file format conversions may involve slight compression, it is kept to a minimum for professional use.
