What is the WebP to PNG Converter?

WebP is a modern, lightweight image format, but it can be difficult to open in older software or certain operating systems. The WebP to PNG converter at HaruTools Lab is a free tool built to solve these compatibility issues by providing high-quality PNG conversions. It is especially ideal for logos and illustrations that feature transparent backgrounds.

Key Features

  • Preserve Transparency: Maintains the alpha channel (transparency) of your original WebP file during the PNG output.
  • Lossless Conversion: Uses canvas rendering to ensure your image quality remains high without any visual degradation.
  • No Installation Needed: Works entirely in your browser, keeping your device safe from potential viruses or malware.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop the WebP images you want to convert into the area above, or use the "Select Files" button.
  2. Click the "Convert to PNG" button.
  3. Click the "Save" button in the list below to download your converted PNG files.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Will transparent backgrounds turn black?

A. No. Our tool supports PNG alpha channels, so transparent areas in your WebP file will remain transparent in the resulting PNG.

Q. Can I convert multiple images at once?

A. Yes! You can select or drag multiple files at the same time to perform bulk conversions instantly.

Q. Does the image quality drop?

A. Since PNG is a lossless format, it preserves the data decoded from the WebP file with high fidelity. There is no noticeable loss in quality after conversion.
