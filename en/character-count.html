What is Character Counter?

Character Counter is a free online tool that lets you quickly measure text length directly in your browser. It's perfect for drafting reports, writing blog posts, checking SNS post limits (like Twitter/X), and even detecting hidden full-width spaces that often cause errors in programming or data entry.

Key Features

  • Real-time Measurement: Every keystroke updates all values instantly.
  • Full-width Space Detection: Specifically identifies and counts hard-to-find full-width spaces.
  • Privacy-Focused: Your text is never sent to a server. Everything happens locally in your browser, making it safe for confidential drafts.
  • No Installation Required: Works seamlessly on PC, smartphones, and tablets via any modern browser.

How to Use

  1. Type or paste the text you want to count into the central text area.
  2. The "Total Characters," "Characters without spaces," "Full-width spaces," "Lines," and "Manuscript Pages (400 chars)" will be calculated automatically.
  3. If needed, drag the bottom-right corner of the text area to resize it.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Are punctuation marks and line breaks counted as characters?

A. Yes. In "Total Characters," line breaks and punctuation are counted as 1 character each. In "Chars (No Spaces)," line breaks and all types of spaces are excluded.

Q. Why is there a count for full-width spaces?

A. In coding, accidental full-width spaces can break the program. It is also useful for checking documents where specific character encoding is required.

Q. Is there a character limit for the input?

A. It depends on your browser's memory, but it handles typical reports and novels up to tens of thousands of characters without any issues.
