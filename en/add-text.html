How to Use the Add Text to Image Tool

This tool is a simple browser-based editor that allows you to add text to images instantly. It is perfect for creating banners, adding captions to SNS posts, or putting dates on your favorite photos.

Key Features

  • Intuitive Interface: Move added text to any location simply by dragging with your mouse or finger.
  • Multiple Text Layers: You can place as many different text layers as you want on a single image.
  • Privacy Focused: Your images are never uploaded to a server. All processing is done locally on your device, making it safe for personal or business photos.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How can I delete text?

A. In the current version, please refresh the page or re-upload the image to start over. An individual layer deletion feature will be added in a future update.

Q. Does it support various fonts?

A. Yes, it utilizes standard system fonts installed on your device for rendering.
