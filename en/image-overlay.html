What is the Image Overlay Tool?

This is an online tool that allows you to layer two images on top of each other and blend them into a single file by adjusting their individual opacities (transparency). Since all rendering occurs locally in your browser, your images are never sent to any server, offering complete privacy and security.

Primary Use Cases

  • Creating Social Media Icons & Banners: Easily place frames or decorative elements over your favorite photos to design original icons.
  • Adding Watermarks (Logos): Protect your copyright by overlaying logo images onto photos and dropping their opacity to serve as a watermark.
  • Design Comparison: Stack a pre-modified and post-modified layout (e.g., Option A vs. Option B) to closely check for misalignments or subtle color variations.
  • Collage & Art Creation: Blend distinct textures or scenery together to simulate fantasy or double-exposure artistic styles.

Technical Benefits & Security

This utility utilizes a client-side approach running entirely in your browser through JavaScript and the Canvas API. This provides prominent benefits that distinguish it from standard image editing cloud services:

  • Total Privacy Protection: Loaded image data resides only within your device's memory (PC or smartphone). It is never uploaded or saved to external servers, making it completely safe for confidential documentation or private family photos.
  • Instant Performance Response: Free from server network communications, the system generates real-time previews synchronized with your slider inputs, as well as immediate download compilations.
  • Offline Compatibility: Once the page is loaded, the tool will function stably even in environments with intermittent or absent internet connectivity.

How to Use It

  1. Click on the "Background (Image 1)" and "Overlay (Image 2)" sections respectively to select your images.
  2. Once the previews display, adjust the sliders left or right to fine-tune your desired transparency levels.
  3. Click the "Blend & Save" button to instantly export and download the combined image in PNG format.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. How are the two images stacked together?

A. You load the base "Background" image first, then add your "Overlay" image (such as a logo or an illustration) second. The overlay image automatically centers itself matching the spatial dimensions of the background image.

Q. Can I adjust the transparency of the overlay image?

A. Yes, you can use the sliders to freely shift opacity between 0% and 100%. This is ideal for subtle watermark configurations.

Q. Can I use transparent PNG files directly?

A. Yes, transparent PNGs are fully supported. Loading logos or graphics with clear backgrounds allows them to neatly combine directly onto the background canvas.

Q. Does combining the images reduce their resolution or quality?

A. No, high image quality is maintained. High-resolution canvas rendering occurs within the browser, letting you export the results in crisp PNG formats.

Q. Are my uploaded files stored on your servers?

A. Not at all. Consistent with other features at "HaruTools Lab", all processing happens locally on your browser. Feel free to compile private images with complete peace of mind.

Q. Does this tool work on mobile devices?

A. Yes, the tool is fully compatible with mobile browsers on iOS and Android. You can intuitively slide to adjust opacity on touch displays.
