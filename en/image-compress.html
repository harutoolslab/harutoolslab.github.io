Why use an Image Compressor?

The "HaruTools Lab" image compressor allows you to drastically reduce image file size while maintaining excellent visual quality. It is perfect for improving website loading speeds, sending emails, or meeting file size limits on social media.

Benefits of this tool

  • Batch Processing: Compress multiple images at once and save them individually.
  • Real-time Adjustments: Fine-tune the compression level using the quality slider.
  • Secure & Private: All processing happens locally in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to any server.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop your images into the area above or click "Select Files".
  2. Adjust the "Quality" slider (we recommend around 0.7 for the best balance).
  3. Click the "Compress Images" button.
  4. Review the new file sizes and click "Download" to save your images.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. How much can I reduce the file size?

A. It depends on the original image, but with a quality setting of 0.7, you can usually expect a 50% to 80% reduction in size.

Q. What file formats are supported?

A. We support major formats such as JPG, PNG, and WebP. Note that all output files will be in high-efficiency JPEG format.
