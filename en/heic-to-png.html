What is the HEIC to PNG Converter?

This is a free online utility that converts HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) images—commonly used by iPhones—into the widely compatible PNG format. You can convert multiple files at once, and since all processing happens inside your browser, your privacy is fully protected.

Benefits of This Tool

  • Completely Free: All features are available without any limits or registration.
  • Bulk Conversion: Process multiple HEIC files into PNGs simultaneously.
  • Privacy-First: Your photo data is never sent to our servers. Everything is processed locally on your device.
  • No Installation: Works instantly on mobile and PC via browsers like Safari, Chrome, and Edge.

How to Use

  1. Drag your HEIC files into the box above or click "Select Files."
  2. Once the files are loaded, click the "Convert to PNG & Download" button.
  3. When conversion is finished, PNG files will be saved automatically (multiple files will download sequentially).
