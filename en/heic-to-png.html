What is the HEIC to PNG Converter Tool?

This is an online utility designed to instantly batch convert HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) files—the default image format used by iPhone cameras—into the highly compatible PNG format. Since this website uses client-side processing technology, your uploaded images are never sent or stored on any external servers, ensuring 100% privacy.

Why Convert HEIC to PNG?

While HEIC is an excellent format for keeping file sizes small while maintaining high quality, it is often incompatible with older Windows photo viewers, certain social media platforms, and blogging systems. Converting your images to PNG optimizes them for viewing on any OS or browser while preserving background transparency data.
