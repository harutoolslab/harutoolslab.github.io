What is the Grid Pixel Art Maker?

This tool is an online editor specialized for creating "Pixel Art," where you place colors precisely pixel by pixel. With a fixed white background and subtle gray grid lines, even beginners can create detailed dot art without getting lost.

Benefits of This Tool

  • Completely Free & No Signup: Start drawing immediately in your browser.
  • Grid Line Display: Helper lines adjust with your zoom level, making it easy to track pixel positions.
  • High-Quality Export: Maintain sharp pixel quality while scaling up to a size perfect for SNS or icons, with transparent PNG support.

How to Use

  1. Select the canvas size (e.g., 32×32) and click the Reset button.
  2. Use the Pen tool and Color Picker to draw on the canvas.
  3. Use "Save Color" to store your favorite colors in the palette.
  4. Choose an export size and click "Save Image" to download your work.
