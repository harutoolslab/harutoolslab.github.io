Q. How can HaruTools Lab remain 100% free?

Q. Is my data really safe from server storage?

A. Yes. Unlike traditional "Cloud" services, we do not have a backend storage system for user files. Your device's hardware performs all the computations. Once you close your browser tab, all traces of the active session are permanently cleared from your RAM.

Q. Do I need to install any software or browser extensions?

A. No installation is required. HaruTools Lab is a "Zero-Install" platform that works natively within modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. This makes it compatible across all operating systems, including Windows, macOS, Linux, and mobile platforms.

Q. Can I process multiple files in a single session?

A. Absolutely. Our image and document tools are optimized for batch processing. You can upload multiple assets simultaneously, allowing our algorithms to process them in parallel using your browser's multi-threading capabilities to save you valuable time.

Q. Will image compression affect my visual quality?

A. We provide granular control over your output. Our intelligent compression algorithms are designed to maximize file size reduction while maintaining visual integrity. For those requiring pixel-perfect results, we offer lossless optimization modes ideal for professional photography and high-end web design.
