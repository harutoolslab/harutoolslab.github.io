Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Is there any cost or daily usage limit?

A. No. Every tool in HaruTools Lab is 100% free to use. There are no "Pro" tiers, hidden subscriptions, or daily limits on how many files you can process. We sustain our lab through minimal, non-intrusive advertisements to keep the lights on without taxing our users.

Q. Are my uploaded files saved on the server?

A. No. We do not have a "backend" storage for your files. Your browser performs all the heavy lifting using your device's CPU and RAM. Once you close the tab or refresh the page, all temporary data is wiped from your device's memory. Your privacy is structurally guaranteed by the very way our site is built.

Q. Can I use it on my smartphone or tablet?

A. Yes, HaruTools Lab is fully mobile-responsive. Whether you are on an iPhone, Android, or iPad, you can access the full power of our tools directly from your mobile browser without installing any suspicious third-party apps.

Q. Can I process multiple images at once?

A. Yes. We currently offer several batch-processing tools specifically for image compression and format conversion. We are constantly updating our other utilities to support multi-file processing to help you save even more time.

Q. Will the quality of the image decrease after compression?

A. Our algorithms are designed to provide the best balance between file size reduction and visual quality. Our "Lossless" options ensure that professional-grade clarity is maintained even after significant size reduction, making them perfect for web developers and photographers.
