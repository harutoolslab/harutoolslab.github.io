What is the PDF Merger Tool?

The "PDF Merger Tool" is a free online utility that allows you to easily combine multiple separate PDF files into a single document directly in your browser. It's perfect for organizing documents scanned separately or combining reports created with different software.

Benefits of This Tool

  • 100% Free & No Registration: Use all features for free, instantly, without creating an account.
  • Enhanced Security: Powered by "pdf-lib" technology, all processing happens locally on your browser. Your files are never sent to an external server.
  • No Installation Needed: Works directly on Windows, Mac, and mobile browsers.

Use Cases for Merging PDF Files

1. Efficient Document Submission: Combine multiple reports or certificates into one PDF to prevent missing attachments in emails and make it easier for the recipient.

2. Organizing Scanned Data: Save materials scanned page-by-page as a single digital book in the correct order.

3. Applications & Contracts: Merge supplementary documents with a main application form to manage everything as a single package.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Can I change the merging order?

A. In the current version, files are listed in the order they are selected (or dragged). To change the order, please reset and add files one by one in your desired order, or number your files (01, 02...) before selecting them all at once.

Q. Can it handle large PDFs with many pages?

A. Most business documents work fine. However, extremely large files (over several hundred MB) might cause errors due to browser memory limits.

Q. Does the quality drop after merging?

A. No. The page data is copied directly into a new container, so the original resolution and text information are preserved.
