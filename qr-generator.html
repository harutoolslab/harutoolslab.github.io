What is the QR Code Generator?

The "QR Code Generator" is a free online service that converts website URLs or any text information into readable QR code images. You can use these codes for flyers, business cards, posters, social media profiles, and more.

Key Features

  • No Registration Required: Use all features for free for both commercial and personal projects without logging in.
  • Privacy First: The generation process happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your data is never sent to a server or saved as a log.
  • Flexible Customization: Adjust the size (128px to 1024px) and color to fit your specific needs.

Ideas for Using QR Codes

1. Boost Social Media Following: Turn your Instagram, TikTok, or X (Twitter) profile URL into a QR code and place it at checkout counters or on tables to encourage instant follows.

2. Business Cards & Portfolios: Add a QR code to your business card linking to your portfolio or contact info, making it easy for people to access your work with one scan.

3. Event Signage: Increase convenience for attendees by linking QR codes to event registration forms or Google Maps locations.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Do the generated QR codes have an expiration date?

A. No, they do not expire. As long as the destination URL remains active, your QR code will work indefinitely. Please note that if you delete or change the URL itself, the QR code will no longer reach the intended page.

Q. Does changing the color affect scannability?

A. Yes, it can. Codes with light colors or those similar to the background color are harder to scan. We strongly recommend a high-contrast combination like "White Background" and "Dark Code." Always test with your own phone before printing.

Q. Is commercial use allowed?

A. Yes, commercial and profit-driven use is permitted without any restrictions.
