What is the Image Flip Tool?

The "Image Flip Tool" is a free online utility that allows you to flip images horizontally or vertically (mirror effect) directly in your browser. No installation is required, and it works seamlessly on smartphones and PCs. It's incredibly useful for fixing the orientation of selfies or checking the balance of a design.

Key Features & Use Cases

  • Flip Horizontal (Mirror): Creates a mirror-like effect. Useful for correcting selfie orientations or creating symmetrical designs.
  • Flip Vertical: Turns the image upside down. Perfect for creative editing or fixing upside-down photos.
  • Privacy Focused: Your image data is never sent to a server. All processing happens entirely within your browser for maximum security.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop an image into the box above, or click "Select File."
  2. Click the "Flip Horizontal" or "Flip Vertical" buttons to achieve your desired orientation.
  3. Click the "Save Flipped Image" button to download your processed image.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Is there any cost?

A. No, this tool is completely free to use with no hidden fees or limits.

Q. Does it change the image quality?

A. We maintain the original resolution as much as possible. Since the output is rendered via Canvas, the image is saved in high-quality PNG format.
