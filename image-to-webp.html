What is the WebP Converter?

The HaruTools Lab WebP Converter is a free utility that converts standard PNG and JPG images into "WebP," a modern image format developed by Google. By using WebP, you can significantly reduce file sizes without losing noticeable quality, which directly contributes to faster page loads and improved SEO performance.

Key Features

  • Batch Conversion: Convert multiple images to WebP format simultaneously.
  • Quality Control: Use the slider to balance file size and visual quality.
  • Secure & Fast: Your images are never sent to a server. All processing happens locally on your device for maximum privacy.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop the images you want to convert into the box, or click the select button.
  2. Adjust the "Quality" slider (0.8 is usually optimal for most web uses).
  3. Click the "Convert to WebP" button.
  4. Click the "Save" link next to each generated filename to download your WebP images.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. What are the benefits of using WebP?

A. WebP files are significantly smaller than JPGs or PNGs. Smaller files mean faster website speeds, which improves SEO rankings and user experience.

Q. Is WebP supported by all browsers?

A. Yes, all modern browsers including Chrome, Safari, Edge, and Firefox fully support the WebP format.
