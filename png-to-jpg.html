What is the PNG to JPG Converter?

The PNG to JPG converter by "HaruTools Lab" is a fast utility designed to change PNG files (which often contain transparency) into highly compatible JPG format. While PNG maintains perfect quality, it can result in large file sizes. Converting to JPG before uploading to blogs or websites helps significantly improve page load speeds.

Key Features

  • Automatic Transparency Handling: Transparent parts of your PNG are automatically filled with white during conversion.
  • Fast Batch Conversion: Process multiple PNG files at once with a single click.
  • Secure Processing: All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never sent to a server.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop the PNG images you want to convert into the area above, or use the select button.
  2. Click the "Convert to JPG" button.
  3. Verify the file names in the list and click "Download" to save your images.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. What happens to transparent backgrounds?

A. JPG format does not support transparency. This tool automatically fills transparent backgrounds with white to complete the conversion.

Q. How much smaller will the file size be?

A. It depends on the image content, but for photographic images, JPGs can often be several times to ten times smaller than PNGs.
