What is the Image Filter Tool?

This is an online tool that allows you to apply various filter effects to your photos directly in your browser. No special software is required, and you can instantly transform your social media icons or website assets into artistic pieces.

Main Available Filters

  • Sepia: Gives your photos a warm, brownish tone like old vintage photographs.
  • Invert (Negative): Completely reverses the colors for a creative, film-negative look.
  • Grayscale: Removes all color to emphasize light and shadow in a classic black and white style.
  • Blur: Softens the image, perfect for background effects or light privacy protection.

How to Use

  1. Select one or more images you want to process.
  2. Choose the desired filter from the dropdown menu.
  3. Click "Apply Filter" to process the images.
  4. Save the processed images using the provided download links.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Does the image quality decrease?

A. We use the browser's Canvas rendering, which maintains high quality suitable for most general uses. Images are exported in transparent PNG format.

Q. Is my privacy protected?

A. Yes. Your images are never sent to a server. All processing is done entirely within your browser (locally on your device's memory), so you can use it with peace of mind.
