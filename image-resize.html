What is the Image Resizer?

The Image Resizer by HaruTools Lab is a free tool designed to convert your images to a specific resolution (pixels). It is perfect for unifying sizes for blog thumbnails or adjusting images to meet social media requirements.

Tool Features

  • No Installation Required: Works entirely in your browser, even on smartphones.
  • Batch Processing: Select multiple images and convert them all to the same size at once.
  • Privacy Protection: Images are never sent to a server; everything is processed locally on your device.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop images into the area or click to select files.
  2. Enter the desired "Width" and "Height" in pixels.
  3. Click the "Run Resize" button.
  4. Click the filename below the resized image preview to save it.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Will resizing reduce image quality?

A. Upscaling images may result in some pixelation, but downscaling uses high-quality canvas rendering to keep quality loss to a minimum.

Q. Is transparency in PNGs maintained?

A. This tool currently outputs images in JPEG format, so transparent areas may be converted to white or black. If you need to keep transparency, please use a dedicated background removal tool.
