What is the Mosaic & Blur Tool?

This tool is a free online utility designed to hide sensitive information in photos, such as faces, license plates, or addresses. You can easily apply "mosaic" or "blur" effects to protect your privacy. No installation is required; it works instantly in your PC or smartphone browser.

Key Features

  • Privacy-First Design: Images are never uploaded to a server. All processing happens locally on your device (browser), making it safe even for confidential information.
  • Adjustable Intensity: Use the slider to control the effect, ranging from a light blur to a heavy mosaic where content is completely unrecognizable.
  • Batch Processing: Upload, convert, and save multiple photos simultaneously to save time.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop images into the area above or click "Select Files" to choose photos.
  2. Adjust the "Intensity" slider. Higher numbers result in a coarser mosaic effect.
  3. Click the "Apply Effect" button to process the images.
  4. Click "Download All" or individual images to save the edited files.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Is there any fee?

A. No, all features are completely free to use.

Q. Are my uploaded images saved on the server?

A. No. This tool works entirely "client-side" within your browser. Your data is never transmitted externally.

Q. What is the difference between mosaic and blur?

A. This tool uses a pixel-averaging method. Lower settings create a natural "blur" look, while higher settings produce a digital "mosaic" appearance.
