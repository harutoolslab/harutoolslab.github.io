What is the WebP to JPG Converter?

Have you ever saved an image from a website only to find it has a ".webp" extension that won't open in your favorite editor or upload to social media? The WebP to JPG converter by HaruTools Lab is a free tool designed to transform those files into JPG, the world's most compatible image format.

Key Features

  • Bulk Conversion: Drag and drop multiple WebP images to convert them all at once.
  • Transparent Background Support: If your WebP has transparency, our tool automatically adds a clean white background during conversion.
  • Privacy First: Your images are never sent to a server. Everything is processed locally in your browser, ensuring your data stays safe and secure.

How to Use

  1. Drag and drop the WebP images you want to convert into the area above, or use the "Select Files" button.
  2. Click the "Convert to JPG" button.
  3. The converted images will appear below. Click the "Download" button for each image to save them.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Does converting WebP to JPG affect image quality?

A. Since JPG is a lossy format, a tiny amount of re-compression occurs. However, this tool uses a high-quality setting (90%), making any quality loss virtually invisible to the naked eye.

Q. Are there any images that cannot be converted?

A. Standard static WebP images convert perfectly. For animated WebPs, the tool will convert the first frame into a static JPG image.
